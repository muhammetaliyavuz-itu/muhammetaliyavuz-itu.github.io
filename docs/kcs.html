Engineering Interpretation

The closest agreement with experimental measurements was obtained using the k-ε model with approximately 1.0M cells and Δt = 0.04 s. Mesh refinement beyond 2M cells did not significantly improve predictive accuracy. Reduced time-step introduced slight underprediction of total resistance.
